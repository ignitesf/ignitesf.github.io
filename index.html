About

Ignite San Francisco is a night of speedy presentations. Presenting on a subject of their choice, speakers have exactly 5 minutes to teach something, enlighten us, or simply inspire but the twist is they have 20 slides that auto-advancing every 15 seconds. This month we're asking our speakers to share part of their world. To show us behind the curtain.

Attend

April 26th 2016

Ignite SF: Behind The Curtain
Doors at 7pm
Marine Memorial Theatre

Get Tickets!

Speak

Ignite is about sharing your interests and passions with the community. Teach us something new, explain something your facinated by, enlighten us but make it quick. Each speaker gets 20 slides and 15 secs per slide. The slides automatically advance. It's a format the keeps things lively and interesting.
